Avenue Gives Back

At Avenue Realty we are grateful to call Charlottesville home. This is why we are excited to be giving back to our community both through volunteerism and monetary donations. To date we have donated funds or goods to the Charlottesville Ballet, Northside Baseball, March of Dimes, Northside Preschool, families of the Albemarle County school district, Salvation Army, Virginia Basketball Academy and Forest Lakes swim team. Volunteer hours have gone to Meals on Wheels and Habitat for Humanity. We are excited to continue to grow this list of worthy non-profits who help shape the Charlottesville community.
